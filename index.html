Who Are We?

Established on 22nd July 2022 Steady is an extracurricular activity at 2 Depok Sleman Vocational High School under the auspices of the Student Council in the skills and entrepreneurship field section. Our slogan "Bring the inside out" encapsulates our commitment to make everyone inside and outside understand more about Stembayo and its features.
